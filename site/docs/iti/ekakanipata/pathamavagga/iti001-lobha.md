# Lobhasutta — Greed / La codicia

*Itivuttaka iti001 · Ekakanipāta, Paṭhamavagga*

> Translated in the style of Bhante Punnaji. Renderings not attested in his published work are reconstructions and are flagged in the notes.

## Text

<span class="segid">iti1:1.1</span>

**Pāli** — Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

**English** — This was said by the Blessed One, said by the Worthy One, so I have heard:

**Español** — Esto fue dicho por el Bienaventurado, dicho por el Digno, así lo he oído:

---

<span class="segid">iti1:1.2</span>

**Pāli** — “Ekadhammaṁ, bhikkhave, pajahatha …

**English** — “Let go of one thing, monks …

**Español** — «Suelten una sola cosa, monjes …

---
